About

Kanku Kala Kendra is a Kathak dance academy in Vadodara, led by the esteemed Guru Smt. Pashmina Vyas. With a passion for preserving the elegance of Kathak, she inspires and nurtures students to master this classical art form. The academy offers a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation, creating a space where talent flourishes.
