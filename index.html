About Us

Kanku Kala Kendra is a vibrant Kathak dance academy with multiple locations across Vadodara. We provide a dynamic and inspiring space where students are guided by expert instructors to master the elegance and rhythm of Kathak. By combining rich traditions with a modern approach, we nurture creativity, passion, and excellence in every dancer, paving the way for a new era of artistic expression.

Events

Join us for spectacular Kathak performances, workshops, and cultural events hosted by Kanku Kala Kendra. Our events bring together passionate artists and enthusiasts to celebrate the art of Kathak in its purest form. Stay tuned for updates on upcoming events and experiences.
