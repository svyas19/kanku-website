About Us

Kanku Kala Kendra is a vibrant and passionate Kathak dance academy dedicated to preserving the essence of this classical Indian art form. Located in the heart of Vadodara, we provide an enriching environment where students can explore their creativity and develop their skills under the guidance of expert instructors. With a perfect blend of tradition and innovation, we aim to inspire and nurture a new generation of Kathak enthusiasts.
