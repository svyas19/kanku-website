About Us

Welcome to Kanku Kala Kendra, a prestigious Kathak dance academy in Vadodara. As the founder and mentor, I, Guru Smt. Pashmina Vyas Upadhyay, am dedicated to preserving the timeless beauty of Kathak while inspiring students to embrace its elegance and depth.

At Kanku Kala Kendra, we welcome students of all ages, from young enthusiasts to seasoned performers. Our academy offers a unique blend of tradition and innovation, fostering an environment where creativity and discipline thrive together.

It is my heartfelt mission to guide every student on their journey, helping them not only master this classical art form but also experience the joy and expression it brings. At Kanku Kala Kendra, we believe in nurturing talent, celebrating tradition, and shaping the future of Kathak.
